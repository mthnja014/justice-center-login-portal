TitleWestern Cape Crime Statistics
CreatorMthiyane, N.M.
DescriptionA thermatic map of Western Cape Crime Statistics. Each police station point is given a colour that correspond to the number of cases found guilty by the court of law. Green points represent police stations that has recorded less 3000 cases that were found guilty by the court of law. Yellow points represents cases greater than or equals to 3000 but less than 6000, orange represent cases greater than or equals to 6000 but less than 9000 cases, and red points represent polices stations that recorded cases greater than or equals to 9000 found guilty by the court of law.
